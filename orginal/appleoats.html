Proteins in Apple Oats Smoothie

  • Oats – Contains plant-based protein (around 2.5–3g per 100g).
  • Milk/Yogurt (if added) – Provides high-quality animal protein (about 3–4g per 100ml).
  • Nuts/Seeds (if included, like almonds or chia seeds) – Add extra protein and healthy fats.

Vitamins in Apple Oats Smoothie

  1. Vitamin C – From apples, boosting immunity and skin health.
  2. Vitamin A – Supports vision and skin regeneration.
  3. B Vitamins (B1, B2, B6, and Folate) – Essential for energy and brain function (from oats, apples, and milk).
  4. Vitamin D (if dairy is used) – Helps with calcium absorption and bone health.
  5. Vitamin E (if nuts/seeds are added) – Works as an antioxidant for skin and cell protection.

This smoothie is a powerful combination of proteins, fiber, and essential vitamins, making it an excellent choice for a healthy breakfast or snack.
